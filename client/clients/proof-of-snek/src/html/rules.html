How to Play

Proof of Snek™ is a card game played with Ethereum where players draw one of four cards at random. Each card drawn gets multiplied by the amount of ETH a player sends. Drawing a Snek Card activates the Countdown timer when at least 5 ETH is staked in the game. Be the last player to draw a Snek Card before the Countdown reaches zero to win the Jackpot.

• WHALE card multiplies ETH by 175%

• BULL card multiplies ETH by 125%

• BEAR card multiplies ETH by 50%

• SNEK card multiplies ETH by 0%

• Snek cards activate the countdown when ETH staked reaches 5 ETH

• Snek cards increase the countdown by 1 hour not to exceed 24 hours

• Last Snek card drawn before the countdown hits zero wins the Jackpot
