How to Play

Proof of Snek™ is a card game played with Ethereum where players draw one of four cards at random. Draw cards to multiply your ETH. Draw a Snek Card to activate the Countdown. Be the last player to draw a Snek Card before the Countdown reaches zero to win the Jackpot.
Each card drawn multiplies the amount of ETH sent
Team Whale • 175%
Team Bull • 125%
Team Bear • 50%
Team Snek • 0%
Draw a Snek card to activate the Countdown

• minimum 5 ETH required to activate

• Snek cards increase countdown by 1 hour not to exceed 24 hours
